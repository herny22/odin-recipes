Old Fashioned

Description

The old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and water, adding whiskey, and garnishing with an orange slice or zest and a cocktail cherry. It is traditionally served with ice in an old fashioned glass.

Ingredients

  • ice
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1-2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 50ml espresso
  • splash of water
  • 60ml Scotch whiskey
  • soda water optional
  • orange slice or peel
  • maraschino cherry optional

Steps

  1. Put the sugar, bitters, and water into a small tumbler. Mix until the sugar dissolves.
  2. Fill a glass with the ice, before stirring in the whiskey and mix from the tumbler. Add a splash of soda water if desired.
  3. Garnish with the orange, as well as the cherry if desired. Serve.
