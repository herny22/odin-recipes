Espresso Martini

Description

The espresso martini is a cold caffeinated alcoholic drink made with espresso, coffee liqueur, and vodka. It is not a true martini as it contains neither gin nor vermouth, but is one of many drinks that incorporate the term martini into their names.

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • 100ml vodka
  • 50ml espresso
  • 50ml coffee liqueur e.g. Kahlua
  • Coffee beans (optional)

Steps

  1. Brew 50ml of espresso-strength coffee.
  2. Add the ice to a cocktail shaker. Pour in the vodka, espresso, and coffee liqueur.
  3. Close up the cocktail shaker, and shake until it feels icy cold.
  4. Strain into a chilled martini glass. Garnish with three coffee beans as desired.
